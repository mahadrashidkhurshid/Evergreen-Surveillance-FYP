Current AQI Data

AQI (Air Quality Index) data is a measure of the quality of the air in a specific location. It is used to inform people about the level of pollutants in the air and their potential health impacts. The AQI is calculated by measuring levels of certain pollutants, including particulate matter, ozone, carbon monoxide, sulfur dioxide, and nitrogen dioxide. The AQI is then presented as a number on a scale, with higher numbers indicating worse air quality. The scale ranges from 0 to 500, with 0-50 considered good air quality and anything above 300 considered hazardous. AQI data can be used to monitor and track air pollution levels and to make decisions on actions to improve the air quality.

Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan, located in the Pothohar Plateau region in the north of the country.
